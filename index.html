Supporting others is fun

There is so much to gain from supporting others in getting to where they need to get

I like flexibility and that flexibility allows me to collaborate and support people who live all around the world. Some of their needs seem straight forward and others not so but I like the challenge and sense of accomplishment I get when they achieve their goals with my help.

Working from home also gives me the opportunity to geek out with the way my surroundings look like. I can go as crazy as I want with colours and when I feel that it's too much, I can always dial that down. It's a nice feeling to have this flexibility.

I learn quickly so even though I might not know something today it doesn't mean I won't know it tomorrow... or next week. Some things require more time than others and I am aware of my limitations and will be upfront with you about it.
